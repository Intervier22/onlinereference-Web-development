The Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a beautiful white marble monument located in the city of Agra on the banks of the river Yamuna.

It is one of the finest examples of architecture that combines elements of mughal,Persian,Ottoman Turkish and Indian architectural styles.

Located in the state of UP,it is one of the Seven Wonders of the world. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahann in memory of his wife,Mumtaz.
